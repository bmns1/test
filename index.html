Volunteering objectives & rules

Tap to expand

Our parent volunteer program is essential to our school community. The objective is to foster a strong partnership between parents and the school. We require each family to complete a set number of volunteer hours per year, divided between two semesters. Withdraw at least three weeks in advance so another parent can take the spot. No-shows incur penalty hours. For safety, please don't bring small children while volunteering. JazakAllahu Khairan!

Parent-Teacher Organization

Building a strong school community together.

As-salamu alaykum! The SVA PTO warmly welcomes all new and returning families. We believe a high level of parent participation is critical to our students' education, tarbiyah, and development. The PTO is a volunteer-based organization of parents, teachers, and staff working together to enhance the SVA experience.

Our mission

  • Enriching events

    International Day, Book Fairs, Movie Nights, Hajj Simulation.

  • Fundraising

    Support school programs, classroom needs, new initiatives.

  • Community

    A collaborative, supportive environment for everyone.

  • Parent voice

    Actively shape your child's journey at SVA.
